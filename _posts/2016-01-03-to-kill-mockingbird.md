This movie is a sequence of lessons to explain what courage really is. And courage is not about heroes, it's about standing up for what's right even when you know you are gone get some and helping the person next to you.

And the first you need to do to show courage is understand why a person is doing what he's doing. "You never really understand a person until you consider things from his point of view... Until you climb inside of his skin and walk around in it."

Finally, two more things. A new word to add to your vocabulary "entailment" and, more on the shallow side, it's a great opportunity to see Robert Duvall with hair.
