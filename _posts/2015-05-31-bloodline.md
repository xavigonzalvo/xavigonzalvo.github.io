[Bloodline](https://www.themoviedb.org/tv/61986-bloodline) is a TV series but it could very well be a proper long movie. The truth is that every family has a black sheep. Maybe we are the one without knowing about it. It's always recurrent, all family members protect themselves and there is someone who doesn't know how to handle pressure and breaks at a young age.

You'll like to watch any [movie](http://www.pajiba.com/seriously_random_lists/eight-cinematic-black-sheep-to-make-you-feel-better-about-your-holiday.php) or book where the weakest person
in a group puts everyone else at risk.
