[The Sacrifice (or Offret)](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/24657-offret) is supposed to be an [apocalyptic nuclear movie](https://www.google.co.uk/webhp?sourceid=chrome-instant&amp;ion=1&amp;espv=2&amp;ie=UTF-8#q=post%20apocalyptic%20movies). But if you really are into that kind of genre note that it is not the "typical" one. Nothing further from reality because it is actually more of a [Binding of Isaac](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Binding_of_Isaac) in a Greek drama way.

It is a movie but it looks like you are in a classic theatre. It is very intense and entertaining specially if you like master interpretations, a close set and low budget (and they spent it all at the end of the movie. You have to watch it to see what I mean!).
