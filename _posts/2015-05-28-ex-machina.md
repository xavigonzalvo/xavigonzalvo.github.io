Excellent special effects and a very good thriller (or love drama?). [Ex-machina](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/264660-ex-machina) brings love to the eternal dilemma or whether artificial intelligence (AI) is really intelligent or not. There's some technical jargon in the movie that makes sense too, so it all sounds more realistic.

My personal opinion is that once we build a good AI system that mimics a human we will have built some sort of human life. It may be imperfect, it may be slightly different but it will have all the flaws and dark thoughts of a human. So we have to be aware that once it's built somebody will say "hey shouldn't those things have rights", that's it for the rest of us. So let's hope it takes a while to build one.

I think I would just add that [I, Robot](https://www.themoviedb.org/movie/2048-i-robot?language=en) is not as good but it's a perfect match.
