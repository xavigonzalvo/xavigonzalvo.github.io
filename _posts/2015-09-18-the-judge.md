What's the fuss about becoming your father? It's just your [fate](http://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/10772145/Youll-be-your-father-at-38.html">You'll be your father at 38).

No movie can go wrong if Robert Duvall has Robert Downey Jr. as son. Some movies are special because they come to touch some taboo topic and this is one of them. Son taking care of old demented father and going back to town to connect with his old but real himself? That's the movie.

[Vera Farmiga](https://www.themoviedb.org/person/21657-vera-farmiga) has something, doesn't she? Yes, you know her. She is the sweet and warm therapist in [The Departed](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0407887/?ref_=nm_knf_t2).
