The Lords of Discipline is an 80s movies with reasonable performance from
[David Keith](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001418/) but that will take you to the sweet age of movies when a well formed story was presented end-to-end with no too much extras and a good wardrobe.

One may say that old movies are always better because they had less tricks to confuse the viewer. Script had to always be up to the standards of the viewers and the performance allowed a bit of exaggeration.
